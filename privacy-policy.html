Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2024

Introduction

Welcome to Mighty Tab. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use our document processing services.

Information We Collect

Personal Information

We collect email addresses that you provide when creating your account. This information is necessary to provide you with access to our services and communicate with you about your account.

Usage Information

We may collect information about how you use our service, including the types of documents you process and your usage patterns to improve our services.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To create and manage your user account
  • To provide our document processing services
  • To communicate with you about your account and services
  • To improve and enhance our services
  • To ensure the security and integrity of our platform

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to provide and improve our services:

Stripe

Payment processing services. We do not store your payment information on our servers.

Google Vertex AI

Document processing and optical character recognition (OCR) services.

n8n

Automation workflows to enhance our service delivery.

Supabase

Database services for secure data storage and management.

Data Storage and Security

Your data is securely stored in our Supabase database with industry-standard encryption and security measures. We implement appropriate technical and organizational safeguards to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: You can view and access your personal information at any time through your account dashboard
  • Edit: You can update and modify your personal information directly in your account settings
  • Delete: You can delete your account and all associated data at any time through your account settings
  • Export: You can export your data in standard formats for your own use

Cookies and Tracking

We do not use cookies or any tracking technologies on our platform. Your browsing experience is not monitored or tracked through cookies.

Data Retention

We retain your personal information only as long as necessary to provide our services and fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy. When you delete your account, all associated data is permanently removed from our systems.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this document.
